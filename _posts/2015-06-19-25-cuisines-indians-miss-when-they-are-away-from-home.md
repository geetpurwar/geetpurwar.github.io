<h2>1. Besan k Ladoo</h2>
Mummy k haath k tasty ladoo can never be replaced!

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/6786_23_86248527_862485.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-633" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/6786_23_86248527_862485.jpg" alt="6786_23_86248527_862485" width="350" height="350" /></a>
<h2>2. Baingan ka Bharta</h2>
The crumbled brinjals and the smoky flavors of Indian homes can never be felt otherwise.

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Baingan-ka-Bharta1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-634" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Baingan-ka-Bharta1.jpg" alt="Baingan-ka-Bharta1" width="600" height="442" /></a>
<h2>3. Arhar ki Daal</h2>
The special tadka and the memorable senses can never be found any where else.

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/634946317287853560_dal_fry.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-635" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/634946317287853560_dal_fry.jpg" alt="634946317287853560_dal_fry" width="1024" height="768" /></a>
<h2>4. Kadhi</h2>
The awesome combo of curd ,curry leaves and the red chilli tadka with the aroma of desi ghee,can you find it in any luxurious restaurants ?

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/640x478_ac.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-636" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/640x478_ac.jpg" alt="640x478_ac" width="640" height="478" /></a>
<h2>5. Chaawal ki Kheer</h2>
The taste of this special kheer with the chopped dry fruits, after cooling it for sometime can never be substituted with any of the desserts until so far...!

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/RiceKheer.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-637" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/RiceKheer.jpg" alt="RiceKheer" width="500" height="375" /></a>
<h2>6. Ghujhiya</h2>
The stuffed khoya with the dry fruits and the special shape given by Mom's hands can never be forgotten where ever we are...

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/image-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-638" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/image-7.jpg" alt="image-7" width="500" height="375" /></a>
<h2>7. Ghar ki Roti</h2>
The tasty and yummy baked roti made by our mother's hand will always be remembered!!!

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/11133705_342409909289231_1620621974050162179_n.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-639" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/11133705_342409909289231_1620621974050162179_n.jpg" alt="11133705_342409909289231_1620621974050162179_n" width="600" height="400" /></a>

The people who like this veggie can never get in the form like home made karela.

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/oetqIhgbddhbe_bigger.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-640" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/oetqIhgbddhbe_bigger.jpg" alt="oetqIhgbddhbe_bigger" width="592" height="279" /></a>

This veggie when cooked in lots of oil and onion gives a special homemade taste which you will never find in any of the cuisines outside of your homes...

The aroma of desi ghee when the halwa is cooked in the home has the power to attract you even from the miles...

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Suji_ka_Halwa_rcpimage_dc5c4ef1-85ca-4c63-a7a4-53edd4712db1_Suji_ka_Halwa_nita1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-643" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Suji_ka_Halwa_rcpimage_dc5c4ef1-85ca-4c63-a7a4-53edd4712db1_Suji_ka_Halwa_nita1.jpg" alt="Suji_ka_Halwa_rcpimage_dc5c4ef1-85ca-4c63-a7a4-53edd4712db1_Suji_ka_Halwa_nita1" width="800" height="370" /></a>

The yummy and fried bhindi along with ghar ka daal chawal will always arise you from deep slumbers and force you towards the dining table.

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/img_2989.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-644" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/img_2989.jpg" alt="img_2989" width="1188" height="792" /></a>
<h2>13. Khichdi</h2>
The amazing blend of desi ghee and moong ki daal which feels very light and delicious when you get the taste of Mom's love!

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/images8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-645" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/images8.jpg" alt="images" width="595" height="446" /></a>
<h2>14. Maal pua</h2>
The desserts of our grand mother's hands and then our Mom's and the sweetness of love it offers can not be found in Baskin Robbin's stuff also.

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/malpua1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-646" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/malpua1.jpg" alt="malpua1" width="3456" height="2304" /></a>
<h2>15. Baati chokha</h2>
The smoke cooked baati and the chokha with smells of ghee all over and the hot servings will also crave you to return back home and take that feel.

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/baati-chokha.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-647" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/baati-chokha.jpg" alt="baati-chokha" width="1600" height="1247" /></a>
<h2>16. Ghar ka Achaar</h2>
The kaccha aam ka fresh achaar on the terrace and we fighting for one bite makes us remember the days we siblings were together and used to have an everlasting scolding with our Mom's!

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Punjabi_Aam_Ka_Achar_foodfood.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-648" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Punjabi_Aam_Ka_Achar_foodfood.jpg" alt="Punjabi_Aam_Ka_Achar_foodfood" width="405" height="315" /></a>
<h2>17. Boondi ka Raita</h2>
The taste of home made curd and the tadka of dry red chillies with sarso in the raita , have you ever tried to compare it with the outside servings?

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/boondi-raitha.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-649" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/boondi-raitha.jpg" alt="boondi-raitha" width="1600" height="1334" /></a>
<h2>18. Dahi Chawal</h2>
When we wanted to eat something light and refreshing , and then we had our great self made dishes of curd and rice , always made us happy and contented!

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Thair-Sadam.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-650" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Thair-Sadam.jpg" alt="Thair Sadam" width="1600" height="1360" /></a>
<h2>19. Bhelpuri</h2>
The blend of different chutneys, with different namkeen and the mixture enhanced with taste of chopped onions and lemon juice , will you ever like to miss that chance away from home?

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/bhel-puri.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-651" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/bhel-puri.jpg" alt="bhel-puri" width="1600" height="1200" /></a>

The weekends and the favorite lunch of different families with curd, salad, papad, chutney will always be missed when away from home.

<a href="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/whole_wheat_paratha.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-653" src="http://www.india25.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/whole_wheat_paratha.jpg" alt="whole_wheat_paratha" width="800" height="600" /></a>
